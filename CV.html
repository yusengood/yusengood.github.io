CV

Summary

My research focuses on numerical solutions to Einstein’s theory of general relativity, in cosmological and astrophysical contexts. I am a dedicated researcher with a strong academic background, and a wide network of international collaborators.

I am a proficient public speaker, communicating my research to specialist and non specialist audiences in a pedagogical manner. I enjoy teaching students and am frequently invited to speak at conferences and outreach events.

I am a member of the GRChombo collaboration, which is developing a new publicly available Numerical Relativity code. For more information and a link to the code go to www.grchombo.org.

I am one of the organisers of the Collabor8 conference for young researchers. For more information go to www.collabor8research.com.
