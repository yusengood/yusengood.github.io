Welcome to the website of Katy Clough!

My research focusses on the behaviour of fundamental fields in strong gravity, in particular the numerical simulation of early Universe physics and the interaction of bosonic fields with black holes. I am passionate about communicating my research to non specialist audiences, in particular with the aim of encouraging underrepresented groups to pursue careers in theoretical physics and cosmology.
